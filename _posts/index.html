About Me

alt="Mohamed Amine Ben Aoun" class="rounded-full shadow-lg border-4 border-blue-400 mx-auto w-48 h-48 object-cover" />

Hello! I'm Mohamed Amine Ben Aoun, a passionate and dedicated cybersecurity professional committed to exploring the intricacies of digital security and building robust defenses. My journey in cybersecurity is driven by a strong curiosity for how systems work, how they can be exploited, and most importantly, how to protect them.

My expertise spans across various domains, including penetration testing, web application security, network security, and security research. I thrive on challenges, constantly learning new techniques and staying ahead of emerging threats to ensure digital safety.

I'm also deeply involved in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, which allow me to sharpen my skills in a practical, hands-on environment, focusing on areas like reverse engineering, exploit development (pwn), and digital forensics. My goal is to make cybersecurity accessible and understandable, contributing to a safer online world.
