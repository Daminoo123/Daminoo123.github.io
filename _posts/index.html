About Me

Hello! I'm Amine, a cybersecurity enthusiast from Tunisia with a deep-rooted passion for understanding and mitigating digital threats. My journey started with a simple curiosity for how things work, which quickly evolved into a fascination with network security and ethical hacking.

I thrive in challenging environments and enjoy participating in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, where I sharpen my skills in cryptography, forensics, and binary exploitation. I am a firm believer in the power of open-source and contribute to security tools whenever possible.

My goal is to leverage my skills to build more resilient and secure digital infrastructures. When I'm not in front of a terminal, I enjoy reading sci-fi novels and exploring the beautiful landscapes of my country.
