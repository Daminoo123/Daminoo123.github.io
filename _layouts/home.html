About Me

Hello! I am Mohamed Amine Ben Aoun, though I also go by Damino. I’m a passionate and dedicated Cybersecurity Engineering student with a strong interest in ethical hacking, system security, and network defense. My journey began with a deep fascination for how systems work at their core and a curiosity about the vulnerabilities that could compromise them. Over time, that curiosity grew into a commitment to understanding and securing digital infrastructures. I enjoy learning by building, breaking, and defending, and I’m constantly exploring new tools, frameworks, and real-world scenarios to sharpen my skills.
